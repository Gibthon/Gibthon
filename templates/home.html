Tools for Synthetic Biology

What

Gibthon is a suite of web-based tools to aid in the design and manufacture of synthetic parts and devices for biological systems.

Why

The 2010 Cambridge iGEM team found a need for an integrated toolkit to automate primer design for Gibson Assembly.

Contribute

Gibthon is an open-source project, and the development team are keen to welcome new members with new ideas. Please email bill@gibthon.org and visit our public repo on GitHub
